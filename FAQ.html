Frequently Asked Questions(F.A.Q.)

  1. The very first question that comes up is....What is Credit Score?
    Credit Score, in simple terms, can be understood as a tool used by banks to make sure that the customer they are lending out loan or credit card are trustable or not.

  2. So how does a credit score help me and why should I take it into consideration before applying for loan or credit card?
    The better the score, the more is the chance you will be eligible for approval of your request. As a matter of fact, banks lend out 80% of their credits to people with a score of 750 or more. So, a good credit score is an indicator of your financial health.
    One should take into consideration before applying just because it saves the hassle of being in uncertainity and also helps you keep record of your finances in case an emergency arises.

  3. What Parameters do we consider for calculating credit score?
